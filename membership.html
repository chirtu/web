Membership

Join TERNET and become part of Tanzania’s trusted network for higher education and research institutions, enabling collaboration, digital transformation, and shared access to advanced ICT services.

Why Become a Member

Empowering Institutions Through Shared Digital Infrastructure

TERNET membership is designed for higher education and research institutions in Tanzania that seek reliable connectivity, secure access to academic resources, and modern digital services.

By joining TERNET, member institutions benefit from a collaborative national network that supports education, innovation, research partnerships, and access to trusted technology platforms.

Our services are tailored to strengthen institutional capacity through high-speed connectivity, federated access, secure remote access, cloud services, and cyber security support.

Key Membership Benefits

  • Access to high-speed institutional connectivity
  • Access to eduroam and Metro eduroam services
  • Secure remote access through eduVPN
  • Cloud and colocation solutions for digital infrastructure
  • Cyber security support and trusted service environment
  • Improved collaboration among education and research institutions

Eligibility

Who Can Join TERNET

Membership is open to eligible higher education institutions, research institutions, and other organizations that support education, innovation, and research in Tanzania. Institutions should be duly recognized, registered, licensed, or accredited by relevant authorities.

Higher Education Institutions

Universities, colleges, and tertiary institutions legally recognized to provide higher education.

Research Institutions

Organizations engaged in scientific research, innovation, knowledge creation, and academic advancement.

Recognized & Accredited

Institutions should meet applicable registration, licensing, endorsement, or accreditation requirements.

Support Institutions

Organizations that complement education and research delivery may qualify under relevant membership categories.

Onboarding Process

How to Become a Member

Our membership process is simple and designed to help institutions join smoothly and begin accessing services efficiently.

1
Express Interest

The institution contacts TERNET and submits an expression of interest for membership and the services it requires.

2
Eligibility Review

TERNET reviews the institution’s status, recognition, and suitability under the applicable membership category.

3
Service Activation

After approval, the institution completes the necessary membership and service documentation to begin onboarding.

Membership Categories

Choose the Right Membership Path

TERNET membership may be offered through different categories depending on the type and legal status of the institution.

Full Membership

Suitable for recognized universities, higher learning institutions, and research institutions.

  • Universities and degree-awarding institutions
  • Recognized higher education institutions
  • Research and innovation institutions
  • Institutions requiring full access to TERNET services

Associate Membership

Suitable for institutions that support or are progressing toward full participation in the network.

  • Tertiary and specialized training institutions
  • Institutions in transition toward full eligibility
  • Public or sector support bodies linked to education and research
  • Organizations with selected service needs

Affiliate Membership

Suitable for institutions and partners that complement education, quality assurance, and knowledge delivery.

  • Support institutions and sector bodies
  • Education-enabling organizations
  • Collaborative partners and special initiatives
  • Institutions seeking selected network participation
