Onboarding Process

How to Join

Three simple steps to become a TERNET member.

01
Express Interest

To be a member of TERNET, an institution expresses interest in a particular service, supported by documents from respective authorities.

02
Eligibility Verification

TERNET verifies the provided information and if the institution is found to be eligible, the institution signs a membership form.

03
Service Documents

For the services the institution is interested in, the institution signs service order forms and agreements provided by TERNET.

FAQ

Questions & Answers

Who can become a TERNET member?

TERNET membership is open to universities, colleges, research institutes, schools, and other educational institutions registered in Tanzania. Each category has specific eligibility requirements.

What are the benefits of membership?

Members enjoy high-speed internet, research databases, cybersecurity services, training programs, collaboration opportunities, and cloud services.

How long does the application process take?

The process typically takes 2-4 weeks from application to service activation, depending on verification requirements.

What documents are required?

Required documents include registration certificate, proof of legal status, authorized representative ID, and relevant accreditation documents.
