3. Membership Terms

TERNET offers three categories of membership:

  • Corporate Membership - For public universities, degree-awarding institutions established by Act of Parliament, and private chartered universities
  • Associate Membership - For NCHE licensed institutions, tertiary institutions recognized by MoES, and research institutions registered by UNCST
  • Affiliate Membership - For examination bodies, secondary schools, and primary schools authorized by MoES

All members must comply with the membership agreement and service level terms. Membership fees are determined based on the category and services required.
